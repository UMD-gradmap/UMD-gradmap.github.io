GRAD-MAP Winter Workshop

📍 Where and When?
The 2024 GRAD-MAP Winter Workshop took place online (over Zoom) in January 2024.
💰 Cost
The Winter Workshop has no fee. Participants only need a stable internet connection.
✅ Eligibility
Open to undergraduates in the U.S. interested in physics/astronomy. Priority is given to students from institutions without access to research, including community colleges. Applications open in November.
🧭 Professional Development
  • Seminars on grad school, internships, and careers in physics and astronomy.
  • Workshops on essays, presentations, and navigating barriers to research.
💻 Scientific Computing with Python
Beginner-friendly Python tutorials using Anaconda and Jupyter notebooks, adapted from the Goddard Python Bootcamp.
🔬 Research Projects
Students carry out mini-projects with UMD mentors in a wide range of fields. Final presentations wrap up the workshop.
🏛️ Tours of Scientific Facilities
Virtual visits to NIST, NASA Goddard, Johns Hopkins APL, and NRAO Green Bank. These institutions offer internship and career opportunities for students.

GRAD-MAP Summer Scholars

📚 Program Overview
Winter Workshop alumni may apply to this 10-week paid summer research program. It expands on the winter experience with full research projects and weekly cohort activities.
🗓️ Application Timeline
Applications for Summer 2024 are now open! Deadline: Friday, March 15 at 5PM ET.
